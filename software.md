We build tools that are available for the whole the Earth science community. All are open-source, most are well-documented and have extensive unit tests. Most use in Landlab.

### DupuitLEM

Open-source hillslope hydrology and landscape evolution model built on Landlab's GroundwaterDupuitPercolator.

[github.com/DavidLitwin/DupuitLEM](https://github.com/DavidLitwin/DupuitLEM)

### Karst LEM

Simple approaches to carbonate landscape evolution with groundwater in Landlab.

[github.com/DavidLitwin/karst-lem](https://github.com/DavidLitwin/karst-lem)

### GroundwaterDupuitPercolator

Landlab component for shallow groundwater flow.

[Landlab GroundwaterDupuitPercolator](https://landlab.readthedocs.io/en/latest/generated/api/landlab.components.groundwater.dupuit_percolator.html)
  
  
### HeightAboveNearestDrainageCalculator

Landlab component for shallow groundwater flow.
 
[Landlab HeightAboveDrainageCalculator](https://landlab.readthedocs.io/en/latest/generated/api/landlab.components.hand_calculator.hand_calculator.html)
  

### Spectral solution for hillslope diffusion

An implicit-spectral solution to hillslope evolution using Landlab tools.

[github.com/DavidLitwin/SpectralDiffusion](https://github.com/DavidLitwin/SpectralDiffusion)
